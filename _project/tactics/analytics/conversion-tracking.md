# Conversion Tracking

A systematic approach to measuring and analyzing user actions that represent business value, from micro-conversions to major goals. This tactic focuses on understanding what drives successful outcomes.

## Impact & Effort

- **Impact**: High
- **Effort**: Medium
- **Timeline**: 2-3 weeks
- **Technical Complexity**: Medium

## Key Benefits

- Measures success
- Identifies bottlenecks
- Guides optimization
- Proves ROI
- Improves decisions
- Validates changes
- Supports growth
- Enables targeting

## Best Practices

### Do's

- Define clear goals
- Track all steps
- Validate data
- Segment users
- Test tracking
- Document setup
- Monitor regularly
- Share insights

### Don'ts

- Track everything
- Ignore context
- Skip validation
- Mix goals
- Assume success
- Neglect setup
- Miss steps
- Forget value
